Our Story

VolunteerHub started as a collaboration between civic technologists and nonprofit coordinators who needed a shared command center.

Through dozens of interviews with directors, volunteers, and municipal partners, we architected a workflow that mirrors real people, not abstract tickets. From the moment someone applies to volunteer to the celebratory messages after an event, VMS provides context, nudges, and automated record keeping.

Human Centered

Signups feel personal and transparent with onboarding checklists and contextual updates.

Operations Ready

Role based controls, smart approvals, and dashboards ease coordinator workload.

Data Inspired

Events, volunteer history, and attendance logs live in the same database, making reporting simple.

Milestones

Highlights from the journey so far.

  • 2019

    Pilot cohort launches with 50 volunteers across three community kitchens.

  • 2021

    Admin console rewritten with accessibility-first components and responsive layout.

  • 2023

    Automations API opens, enabling SMS nudges, calendar sync, and CRM exports.

  • Today

    Universities and NGOs turn to VMS to coordinate civic action within days.
